Cookies and Other Technologies

We use cookies and other technologies, such as pixel tags, and clear gifs, to personalize our Services to your preferences and to provide an automatic log-in process. Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive. Like many sites, we use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site.

We may partner with a third party ad network to either display advertising on our website or to manage our advertising on other sites. Our ad network partner may use cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information about your activities on this and other websites to provide you targeted advertising based upon your interests. If you wish to not have this information used for the purpose of serving you targeted ads, you may opt-out by contacting us support@cityhapps.com . Please note this does not opt you out of being served advertising. You will continue to receive generic ads.
